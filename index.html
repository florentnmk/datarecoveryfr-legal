DataRecoveryFR Content Studio

A content automation platform for tech and data recovery educators on TikTok.

About

DataRecoveryFR Content Studio is a web-based platform that helps content creators in the data recovery and digital forensics space produce and publish educational short-form videos to TikTok. The platform automates the content production workflow while keeping creators in full control of what gets published.

How it works

  • Creators authenticate via TikTok Login Kit
  • The platform generates educational video content on their chosen topic
  • Videos are uploaded to the creator's TikTok inbox for review before posting
  • Creators approve and publish directly from TikTok
